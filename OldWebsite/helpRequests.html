This page shows your requests for this year.
Select: Click the Select link to the left of each record to enable viewing, editing, or deleting.
View Details: Click the View Details link to the left of each record to view the EO details in another window.
Edit: Click the Edit link to the left of each record to prioritize your selection, request a Field Partner, or enter special instructions. After editing, you must click Update to save your changes. You may not edit EOs that have been assigned or submitted.
Preference Order: Rank your selections in order of preference. Click the Edit link and then enter a number in the Order column. Click Update to save your changes.
Request Field Partner: Check the box to request a field partner for a specific assignment.
Comments: Enter any special instructions.
Submit Items to Rare Care: Click to send requests to Rare Care. Only unassigned items you select will be sent.
